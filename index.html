ACADEMICS

CONVENTIONAL SECTION

At H.I.A Conventional Section, we are committed to nurturing students through a holistic educational approach that integrates Islamic morals and respect for cultural and societal values. Our curriculum emphasizes the importance of fluency in the English language, effective communication skills, and the development of creative and self-reliant abilities. By combining conventional and Islamic teachings, we provide a balanced foundation for sound Western education. Our program aims to foster moral integrity, presentable character, critical thinking, and logical judgment. We also strive to instill a sense of respect and love for one's country, promoting harmonious coexistence and adherence to laws.

TAHFEEZ/ISLAMIYYA SECTION

At H.I.A Tahfeez & Islamiyyah Sections, we are dedicated to instilling Islamic morals and respect for cultural and societal values. Our goal is to build a cohesive Muslim community through love, teaching, and practice. We focus on ensuring children become fluent in Arabic and communicate effectively, along with mastering the memorization and recitation of the Holy Qur'an. Our curriculum includes short-term training in reading and writing skills, fostering moral development, and promoting presentable character. We provide a well-designed curriculum that ensures every individual has the right to learn and grow. Additionally, we emphasize critical thinking and logical judgment, and we operate an integrated system with conventional schools.

TARTEEL CLASSES

At H.I.A Tarteel Classes, we are dedicated to providing a focused and comprehensive Qur'anic education. Our program emphasizes 100% attention to sound recitation methods, ensuring students complete their recitation training in the short term. We incorporate Arabic language input to enhance understanding and proficiency. Additionally, our H.I.A Digital Skills Acquisition Programme offers essential training in computer skills and digital marketing, equipping students with valuable competencies for the modern world.

HIA Home Of Reciters And Jurisprudence

H.I.A Home of Reciters And Jurisprudence Knowledge (Female Adults Only): Offering comprehensive 2-3 year courses with flexible morning and evening classes, focusing on Quran recitation, essential jurisprudence topics, and practical activities to enhance theoretical knowledge.

HIA Holiday Based Operational Services

H.I.A Holidays Based Operational Services offers a specially designed program for weak learners, boarding, and visiting children. It aims to help parents utilize termly school breaks for their kids effectively. The program is open to both internal and external pupils/students, providing proper essential treatment for selected topics through a short-term intensive focused study period.

HIA Centre For Qur'an an Arabic Studies Male Adults Only

The H.I.A Centre for Qur'an and Arabic Studies offers a specialized program for male adults only, featuring basic and advanced certificate courses designed in twin semesters. The program is structured to accommodate students with the convenience of weekend or working day classes, as well as morning and evening sessions, all within a two-year study duration plan.
